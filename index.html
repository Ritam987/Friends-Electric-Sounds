Who Are We?

At Friends Electric & Sound, we don’t just rent equipment — we create the perfect setup for your celebration. Whether it’s light, sound, power, or stage design — we bring it all together under one roof.From wedding mandaps to dance floors with DJs, from fan-cooler setups to professional technicians — we handle the entire event infrastructure, so you can sit back and enjoy your special day.With over 10 years of hands-on excellence in the field, Friends Electric has proudly powered 2000+ successful events across the Bardhaman district. From weddings to cultural shows, we know exactly what it takes to make your celebration shine — on time, on point, and just the way you imagined.
